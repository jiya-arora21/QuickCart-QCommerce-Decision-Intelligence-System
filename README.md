# QuickCart: Quick-Commerce Operations & Customer Experience Decision Intelligence System

## Project Overview

QuickCart is an end-to-end decision intelligence project focused on analyzing quick-commerce operations, delivery performance, platform efficiency, city-level bottlenecks, customer experience, and business improvement opportunities.

The project goes beyond basic dashboarding by combining Python-based data analysis, SQL-driven business queries, Power BI KPI monitoring, and decision intelligence frameworks to generate actionable recommendations for quick-commerce platforms.

## Business Context

Quick-commerce platforms such as Blinkit, Swiggy Instamart, and JioMart compete on speed, reliability, customer satisfaction, product availability, and operational efficiency.

In this highly competitive market, even small delays, poor ratings, or city-level bottlenecks can directly affect customer experience, repeat usage, and platform trust.

This project aims to analyze operational and customer experience data to support better business decisions.

## Business Problem

The core business problem is:

**How can quick-commerce platforms improve delivery performance, reduce late delivery risk, identify city-level bottlenecks, and improve customer experience using data-driven insights?**

This project focuses on answering questions such as:

- Which platform performs best across delivery performance, ratings, and customer experience?
- Which cities show the highest operational bottlenecks?
- How does delivery time impact customer satisfaction?
- Which operational factors contribute most to late delivery risk?
- Which locations or platforms require immediate business attention?
- What actions can improve customer retention and service reliability?

## Objectives

- Analyze quick-commerce order and delivery performance
- Compare platform-level efficiency across Blinkit, Swiggy Instamart, and JioMart
- Identify late delivery patterns and operational bottlenecks
- Measure customer satisfaction using ratings and experience indicators
- Build KPI monitoring for business performance tracking
- Create a Power BI dashboard for executive-level decision-making
- Develop a decision intelligence layer with recommendations and risk insights
